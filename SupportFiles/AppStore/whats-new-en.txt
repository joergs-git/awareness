What's New in v2.1:

- Fixed sync loop between iPhone and Apple Watch — iOS is now the master scheduler, eliminating potential runaway synchronization
- macOS: ESC key now properly dismisses the blackout (was blocked by the keystroke suppression layer). Mouse click also dismisses.
- End flash (white blink visible through closed eyelids) is now enabled by default on iOS and watchOS
- watchOS: Reversed haptic signals — gentle pulses invite you into the blackout, stronger pulses signal the end
