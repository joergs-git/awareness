Atempause gently reminds you to stop, breathe, and observe — your thoughts, your body, this moment. At random intervals throughout your day, it invites you to pause and return to awareness.

In the Vipassana meditation tradition, awareness (sati) is the foundation of all practice. Yet we move through our days on autopilot, lost in thought, gradually losing contact with ourselves. Atempause interrupts this pattern with brief moments of stillness woven into your day.

A few times per hour, you receive a gentle reminder. For a few seconds, there is nothing to do. Close your eyes. Feel your breath. Notice your thoughts. Observe your posture. Then you continue — with a small gap of clarity inserted into your day.

KEY FEATURES

- Random intervals between pauses (e.g. 15–30 minutes), so reminders feel natural, not mechanical
- Configurable duration (3–120 seconds per pause, with optional random variation)
- Smart detection — automatically skips when your camera or microphone is in use
- Active time window — only reminds during configured hours
- Visual modes — plain black, custom text, image, or looping video
- Start and end gong — audible tones mark the beginning and end of each pause
- Handcuffs mode — commit to the practice by preventing early dismissal
- Mindful Moments — donut chart, daily and lifetime stats, 14-day history
- Apple Health integration — log each pause as Mindful Minutes
- Haptic feedback and end flash options on iOS
- Taptic Engine feedback on Apple Watch
- Coordinated scheduling — iPhone and Apple Watch stay in sync
- Cross-device progress — stats merge between paired devices
- Snooze — pause for 10 minutes up to 2 hours, or indefinitely
- Localized in English and German

APPLE WATCH

The companion watchOS app works standalone or paired with your iPhone. Coordinated scheduling keeps reminder times in sync between devices, and your practice stats merge automatically. Receive gentle haptic taps on your wrist, view your mindful moments, and customize settings right from your watch face with WidgetKit complications.

CROSS-PLATFORM

Atempause is also available for macOS and Windows, keeping your practice consistent across all your devices.

This is not a productivity tool. It is a practice aid — micro-interruptions that become anchors of awareness threaded through your day. A reminder to breathe, to observe, to come back.

Breathe. You are here.
