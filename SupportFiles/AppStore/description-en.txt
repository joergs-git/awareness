Awareness reminder is a mindfulness timer that randomly blacks out your screen for a few seconds, gently reminding you to pause, breathe, and return to the present moment.

In the Vipassana meditation tradition, awareness (sati) is the foundation of all practice. Yet we spend hours staring at screens, gradually losing contact with ourselves. Awareness reminder interrupts this pattern with brief moments of stillness woven into your day.

A few times per hour, your screen fades to black. For a few seconds, there is nothing to do. Close your eyes. Feel your breath. Notice your posture. Then the screen returns, and you continue — with a small gap of clarity inserted into your day.

KEY FEATURES

- Random intervals between blackouts (e.g. 15–30 minutes), so interruptions feel natural, not mechanical
- Configurable duration (3–120 seconds per blackout, with optional random variation)
- Smart detection — automatically skips when your camera or microphone is in use
- Active time window — only interrupts during configured hours
- Visual modes — plain black, custom text, image, or looping video
- Start and end gong — audible tones mark the beginning and end of each pause
- Handcuffs mode — commit to the practice by preventing early dismissal
- Progress tracking — donut chart, daily and lifetime stats, 14-day history
- Apple Health integration — log each pause as Mindful Minutes
- Haptic feedback and end flash options on iOS
- Taptic Engine feedback on Apple Watch
- Coordinated scheduling — iPhone and Apple Watch stay in sync
- Cross-device progress — stats merge between paired devices
- Snooze — pause for 10 minutes up to 2 hours, or indefinitely
- Localized in English and German

APPLE WATCH

The companion watchOS app works standalone or paired with your iPhone. Coordinated scheduling keeps notification times in sync between devices, and your practice stats merge automatically. Receive gentle haptic taps on your wrist, view your progress, and customize settings right from your watch face with WidgetKit complications.

CROSS-PLATFORM

Awareness reminder is also available for macOS and Windows, keeping your practice consistent across all your devices.

This is not a productivity tool. It is a practice aid — micro-interruptions that become anchors of awareness threaded through your working day.
