# awareness
A little tool helping staying aware while you are working on your computer. You might even want to call it a breathing or vipassana timer.

This version is a mac osx tool, a iphone/ipad or windows version might follow later.

Being mentally aware and just "be" is helpful for lot's of reason. If you are working on the computer a lot, like me,
you might like to use this little tiny helper.

The idea is to simply blackout your display for a couple of seconds, in random moments, while you are actually working on the computer.
So you are forced to interrupt. Breathe. Gather yourself. And continue a couple of seconds later.

Of course this tool will detect if camera or microphone are in use, so you don't have to be concerned that this happens in a online meeting etc.

You can define the following settings and parameters to customize the behaviour:
- Active Time-Window for YingYang Moments. e.g.  8:00 until 19:00
- Default Interruption duration in seconds: e.g. 10s - means that the screen will be blackedout for this duration
- Visual: Just plain black, or a custom text message, or a nice little picture, or a little tiny video animation to show during blackout.
- Min. and Max. delay during which YingYang will be shown. e.g. Min: 5minutes // Max. 60 Minutes. This means that anytime between 5 minutes up to 60 minutes after the last awareness interruption this tool will randomly blackout your screeen.
- Awareness Gong Sound: On/Off
- Handcuffs-Mode: On=you can't interrupt the blackout duration until it's over. Off=just pressing the ESC or CMD-Q allows you to end it right away so you can continue your work.

After starting this tool, a tiny yingyang icon will be shown in the menu bar. There you can either change some settings or simply quit the app to totally disable it.
