# Awareness — Support

## Getting Help

Awareness is an open-source mindfulness timer for macOS, Windows, iOS/iPadOS, and Apple Watch. If you need help, this page covers the most common questions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Notifications aren't appearing (iOS/watchOS)

Make sure notification permissions are granted:

1. Open **Settings** > **Notifications** > **Awareness**
2. Enable **Allow Notifications**
3. Ensure **Banners** or **Alerts** are selected
4. Return to Awareness and wait for the next scheduled time

If notifications still don't appear, try closing and reopening the app — this triggers a fresh scheduling cycle.

### Blackouts aren't triggering

- **Check the active time window** — blackouts only occur during your configured hours (default: 06:00–20:00). Outside this window, nothing will happen.
- **Check if snooze is active** — look for the snooze indicator in the app. Resume from the menu to cancel snooze.
- **Camera/microphone in use** (macOS/Windows) — Awareness automatically skips blackouts during video calls and meetings.

### How do I set up Apple Health? (iOS/watchOS)

1. Open Awareness > **Settings**
2. Enable **Apple Health**
3. When prompted, grant Awareness permission to write Mindful Minutes
4. Each completed blackout will now be logged as a mindful session

You can verify in the Health app under **Browse** > **Mindfulness** > **Mindful Minutes**.

### My Apple Watch isn't syncing with my iPhone

- Make sure both apps are installed and have been opened at least once
- Check that Bluetooth is enabled on both devices
- Settings sync happens via WatchConnectivity — it may take a moment after changing a setting
- Try opening both apps simultaneously to trigger a sync

### How do I change settings?

- **macOS**: Click the yin-yang icon in the menu bar > **Settings...**
- **Windows**: Right-click the tray icon > **Settings...**
- **iOS**: Tap the gear icon on the home screen
- **watchOS**: Scroll down on the main screen and tap **Settings**

### How do I uninstall Awareness?

- **macOS**: Drag `Awareness.app` from Applications to Trash
- **Windows**: Delete the application folder or uninstall via system settings
- **iOS/watchOS**: Long-press the app icon and select **Delete App**

Found a bug? Please report it on GitHub:

When reporting, please include:
- Which platform (macOS, Windows, iOS, watchOS)
- Your OS version
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Steps to reproduce the issue
