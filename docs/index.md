# Awareness reminder — Mindful Pauses for Your Day

## A Vipassana-Inspired Timer for Screen Workers

We spend hours staring at screens — answering emails, writing code, scrolling feeds — and gradually lose contact with ourselves. We forget to breathe deeply. We forget we even have a body.

**Awareness reminder** interrupts this pattern. A few times per hour, your screen gently fades to black. For a few seconds, there is nothing to do. Close your eyes. Feel your breath. Notice your posture. Then the screen returns, and you continue — with a small gap of clarity inserted into your day.

## How It Works

1. **Install and launch** — Awareness reminder runs quietly in your menu bar or system tray
2. **Go about your day** — at random intervals, your screen fades to black for a few seconds
3. **Pause and breathe** — then continue with renewed awareness

## Features

- **Random blackout intervals** — configurable min/max range so interruptions feel natural
- **Configurable duration** — 3 to 120 seconds per blackout, with optional random variation
- **Smart detection** — automatically skips during video calls and meetings
- **Active time window** — only interrupts during your configured hours
- **Visual modes** — plain black, custom text, image, or looping video
- **Start and end gong** — audible tones mark each pause
- **Handcuffs mode** — prevent early dismissal and commit to the practice
- **Progress tracking** — donut chart, daily stats, and history bar chart
- **Apple Health integration** — log pauses as Mindful Minutes (iOS/watchOS)
- **Coordinated scheduling** — iPhone and Apple Watch stay perfectly in sync
- **Cross-device progress** — practice stats merge between paired devices
- **Haptic feedback** — vibration on iOS, Taptic Engine on Apple Watch
- **Watch face complications** — see your status directly on your watch face
- **Snooze** — pause for 10 minutes up to 2 hours, or indefinitely
- **Localization** — English and German
